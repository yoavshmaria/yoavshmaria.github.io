Search actions HSBC should take

Not more ads everywhere — more ads where intent and topic-fit are highest.

A
Rebuild paid search around mortgage-intent topics

Competitors dominate calculators, borrowing amount, remortgage and rate comparison. HSBC already earns organic mortgage traffic; it should convert that authority into more paid + organic-assisted acquisition.

B
Reduce repetitive loan ad variants

HSBC’s loan ads are heavily calculator-led and near-duplicative. Consolidate copy, test more proposition-led messaging, and free budget for under-covered growth topics.

C
Turn support and rates pages into next-step engines

High-traffic pages like online banking, contact, mortgage rates and savings guides should carry stronger, compliant next-best-action modules: calculator, compare, apply, book a conversation.

90-day action plan

Prioritize fast ROI wins first, then compound with creative and AI/discovery improvements.

Days 1–30
  • Stand up a dedicated mortgage acquisition program: affordability, borrowing, remortgage, first-time buyer, overpayment.
  • Audit HSBC paid search duplication in loans and consolidate into fewer, clearer ad families.
  • Create switching-topic landing pages with stronger next-step UX.
  • Tighten display placement lists: prioritize finance, comparison, home-buying, premium news, travel-affluent.
Days 31–60
  • Launch mobile-first landing page variants for mortgage calculators, switching and global money.
  • Expand answer-led content for AI discovery with schema, FAQs and calculator jumps.
  • Build partner packages for comparison, broker and open banking ecosystems.
  • Retool display creative by journey: home, switcher, global money, business startup, premier.
Days 61–90
  • Shift budget using measured efficiency, not fixed allocations.
  • Introduce sequential messaging: explainer → calculator → application.
  • Roll out assisted conversion reporting for AI/content and comparison referrals.
  • Scale the strongest winning topic clusters and pause weak-context inventory.

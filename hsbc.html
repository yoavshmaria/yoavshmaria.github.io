Top ROI Levers

Priority-ranked growth opportunities based on competitive gap analysis

ROI lever prioritisation matrix
Impact vs effort — size represents estimated traffic uplift potential
01
Critical gap
Scale paid search 6×
HSBC: 30.8K visits · Barclays: 194.6K visits. Target keywords: "mortgage rates uk", "mortgage calculator", "best current account uk", "switch bank account". Quick wins: conquest Barclays/NatWest brand terms. Est. uplift: +150–200K visits/month at full parity.
02
High impact
Fix engagement depth
6.9 pages/visit vs Barclays 8.8 — a 27% gap. Every +1 page/visit at conversion rates translates directly to more applications. Priority: link mortgage calculator → product pages → apply flow. Add contextual CTAs on all top-10 organic landing pages.
03
Medium impact
Build email lifecycle programme
HSBC email: 3.3K desktop visits · Barclays: 13.9K (Jan '25). Barclays surged 4× from Nov→Jan with a "new year finances" campaign. Launch lifecycle sequences: new account (30/60/90 days), cross-sell (savings → mortgage → ISA), annual review. Email delivers highest ROI in FS.
04
Medium impact
Expand mortgage content hub
Top 3 organic pages are all mortgage-related: /mortgages/our-rates (50.9K), /mortgages/repayment-calculator (48K), /mortgages/calculators (31K). These alone = 130K organic clicks. Add: first-time buyer guides, remortgage guides, rate comparison tables, stamp duty calculator. Capture mid-funnel intent at zero marginal CPA.
05
Quick win
AI search optimisation
ChatGPT.com is already a top-10 referrer (4.4K visits). As AI-powered search scales, being the primary cited source for banking queries could become a major channel. Add structured FAQ schema, clear product comparison content, and direct answer blocks to high-traffic pages. Low effort, high future upside.
06
Quick win
Activate social traffic
Social drives only 1.9K desktop visits — essentially zero. While direct social ROI in FS is complex, targeted LinkedIn campaigns for business banking products and YouTube/Instagram for mortgage/savings content builds brand-direct traffic and reduces long-term reliance on branded search. Start with LinkedIn for SME banking.

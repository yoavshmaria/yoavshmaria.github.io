ℹ️
HSBC display visits have declined 18% from Sep '24 (6.4K) to Jan '25 (5.2K), while NatWest has maintained a flat ~4K/month. All three banks treat display as a minimal spend channel — focus should be on increasing publisher quality and reach rather than raw volume.
1
Critical gap
Activate NerdWallet & money.co.uk
Barclays generates 5,556 visits from NerdWallet and 4,386 from money.co.uk. These are high-intent research audiences actively comparing products. HSBC has zero presence on both. Priority placement on mortgage, current account, and credit card comparison pages would capture prospects mid-decision.
2
High impact
Launch Rightmove mortgage display campaign
Rightmove has 100M+ monthly visits from active home buyers and sellers — the highest-intent mortgage audience in the UK. Barclays is already there. HSBC's mortgage tools (calculator, rates page) make this a natural fit. A Rightmove display campaign targeting "first-time buyer" and "remortgage" searches could deliver high-quality mortgage leads at scale.
3
Medium impact
Add uSwitch and Experian to publisher mix
uSwitch audiences are actively switching financial products — perfect for HSBC's current account and credit card offers. Experian users are credit-score aware and actively shopping for products. Barclays is present on both. Adding both publishers would improve acquisition quality vs the gaming/misc sites currently in HSBC's publisher tail.
4
Medium impact
Maintain & grow MSE/MoneySuperMarket presence
HSBC's strongest publisher channels (MSE 37.4%, MoneySuperMarket 26.3%) are both declining year-on-year. These are HSBC's highest-quality display referral sources. Invest in maintaining editorial presence, sponsoring product comparison tables, and securing featured listings — especially for savings accounts and mortgages during rate-change cycles.
5
Quick win
Activate finder.com for international accounts
NatWest generates 572 visits from finder.com — a platform heavily used by expats and internationally mobile consumers. HSBC's international/expat account is its #1 paid search page (2,407 clicks). Extending this campaign to finder.com display placements would reach the same audience in a research-first environment.
6
Quick win
Clean up low-quality publisher tail
HSBC's display publisher list includes bloxd.io (gaming), typing.com, elvebredd.com, topsportfeed.com, and papasgaming.com — clearly irrelevant audiences with near-zero conversion intent. Blocking these placements would redirect budget to higher-quality publishers without increasing total spend. This is free efficiency improvement.

Top ROI Levers

Priority-ranked growth opportunities based on paid & display competitive gap analysis

ROI lever prioritisation matrix
Impact vs effort — bubble size = estimated monthly traffic uplift
01
Critical · Paid Search
Launch mortgage paid search campaign
HSBC has ZERO paid search spend on mortgages. Barclays spends ~£43.5K/month on mortgage keywords (29K clicks), NatWest ~£61.5K (20K clicks). Target: "mortgage calculator", "mortgage agreement in principle", "best mortgage rates UK", "first-time buyer mortgage". Est. uplift: +20–30K paid visits/month.
02
Critical · Paid Search
Conquer current account switching terms
Barclays' #1 paid page gets 55.3K clicks/month on current account keywords — HSBC has no equivalent. Terms like "switch bank account", "best current account UK", "free bank account" represent direct customer acquisition. The 7-day switch guarantee makes this highly competitive on conversion.
03
High · Display
Activate NerdWallet + money.co.uk display
Barclays gets 5,556 visits from NerdWallet and 4,386 from money.co.uk — both zero for HSBC. These are research-first, high-intent audiences comparing financial products. Securing display placements on mortgage, current account, and savings comparison pages would add ~8–10K high-quality visits/month.
04
High · Display
Rightmove mortgage display campaign
Rightmove's home buyer audience is the highest-intent mortgage prospect pool in the UK. Barclays is already present. HSBC's mortgage tools rank organically for "mortgage calculator" — a Rightmove display campaign would complete the funnel by reaching buyers at the point of property search, before they've chosen a lender.
05
High · Paid Search
Scale ISA + investments paid campaign
HSBC runs 472 clicks/month on ISA keywords vs Barclays' 4,302. The April tax year-end is the peak ISA season — HSBC should be scaling paid campaigns significantly from January onwards targeting "stocks & shares ISA", "cash ISA", "best ISA rate". Barclays' Smart Investor campaigns show this is a high-spend priority for competitors.
06
Quick Win · Display
Block low-quality display publishers
HSBC's display publisher tail includes gaming sites (bloxd.io, papasgaming.com) and unrelated content sites with zero financial audience relevance. Blocking these wasted impressions and reallocating to MSE, MoneySuperMarket, NerdWallet, and money.co.uk would immediately improve display ROI with no budget increase.

🚀 Top 6 Strategic Opportunities

1. Organic Search Gap vs. Asana
Asana generates 2× more organic traffic (1.7M/mo) vs. Monday (869K). Key opportunity in project management informational content, comparison keywords, and CRM landing pages.
2. PPC Competitor Bidding Over-investment
Monday bids on Google Calendar, Google Sheets, and generic tools — low-intent keywords. Reallocate toward bottom-funnel "project management software" and vertical-specific terms.
3. CRM as Growth Engine
monday.com/crm ranks well organically (pos.1 for "crm software"). Scale PPC and content investment here — separate brand narrative from PM competitors.
4. Social is Underutilized
Social drives only 0.6% of traffic vs. 0.7% for Asana. January 2025 shows a +90% social spike — replicate whatever worked and build a consistent content calendar.
5. Referral & Affiliate Partnerships
Forbes (+77% YoY), Joinhandshake (+60%), and app.process.st (+162%) are high-growth referral sources. Formalize affiliate programs and expand review site presence.
6. AI Content as Traffic Driver
The blog/ai-agents/ path is already a top-15 organic landing page. Build a dedicated AI content hub — AI tools, comparisons, and use-case guides — to capture emerging search demand.
